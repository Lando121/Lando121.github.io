About

The idea behind Flowi is to give the player a new take on the old endless runner concept. The player is given the ability to affect their own gameplay as they play. This thanks to the dynamic difficulty that is implemented into the game. It's simply a fork in the path, where the player can chose to either take the easier path for lesser reward or the more difficult one for higher reward.
We were adamant during development that the player should instantly feel the weight of their choice, so the color scheme is different in the harder path. Also the spawnrates for collectables and enemies is much higher than in the easier one, and once you've decided to increase the diificulty then you cannot go back. So the game challanges the player to not just be quick in their reactions, but also to evaluate their own positions as they play the game. To help them succeed by strategy aswell as skill.
